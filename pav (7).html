FAQ

Questions people ask

What is this actually for?
Turning typed notes, journal entries, or reflections into a printable page that looks and feels handwritten — for people who like the aesthetic of handwritten notes but type faster than they write.
Can I use this for assignments or homework?
No — PAV isn't built or intended for making typed or AI-written work look handwritten in order to pass it off as your own handwriting for grading or submission. It's meant for personal notes, journaling, and print-friendly formatting.
Does it upload my notes anywhere?
No. Page generation happens entirely in your browser using canvas rendering — nothing is sent to a server.
Can it read my handwriting from a photo?
Yes — upload a photo or PDF and PAV runs on-device OCR to pull out the text automatically, then re-renders it in a handwriting style. It's most accurate on clear, well-lit, typed or neatly written pages; messy cursive may need a quick manual cleanup after extraction.
